Title: Challenge 06: **Countdown**
Description: In this challenge, you are tasked to create a C# program wherein given a specific birthday input (i.e. 02/17/2003), you have to calculate how many days left until that birthday occurs. Note that the current day, no matter what time it is, should not be included.
